# Requirements Artifacts: Ecommerce System

---

## A. Screen Descriptions

### Screen 1: Home Page (Landing)
**Purpose:** Serve as the main entry point for users to browse and discover products in the electronics store.

**Key UI Components:**
- Site header: "Electronics Store" branding
- Navigation menu: Home | Products | Cart
- Welcome banner with promotional text
- "Shop Now" call-to-action button
- "Shop by Category" section with category buttons (Laptops, Headphones, Gaming)
- "Featured Products" section displaying product cards (Product 1, Product 2, Product 3) with prices and "Add to Cart" buttons
- "Special Offer" section advertising 30% off selected items with "View Deals" button

**Main User Interactions:**
- Navigate between sections using top menu
- Browse products by category
- Add featured products to cart
- View special promotional deals

---

### Screen 2: Shopping Cart
**Purpose:** Display selected items and allow users to review, modify, and proceed with their purchase.

**Key UI Components:**
- Header: "Shopping Cart" with navigation (Home | Products | Cart)
- "Selected Items" section with table columns: Product, Qty, Price, Action
- Product line items (e.g., Laptop, Headphones with quantities and prices)
- Remove action buttons for each item
- Subtotal, Tax, and Total calculations
- "Continue Shopping" button
- "Proceed to Checkout" button

**Main User Interactions:**
- Review cart contents
- Remove items from cart
- View cost breakdown
- Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

---

### Screen 3: Products Page (Slideshow/Catalog)
**Purpose:** Display detailed product catalog with search and promotional information.

**Key UI Components:**
- Header: "Electronics Store" with navigation (Home | Products | Cart)
- Product slideshow/carousel area
- "Featured Products" list with individual items (Laptop $899, Wireless Mouse $25, Headphones $75, Smart Watch $199)
- "Add to Cart" buttons for each product
- "Search Products" input field with search button
- "Promotions" section displaying "Free shipping on orders over $100"

**Main User Interactions:**
- Browse product slideshow
- Search for specific products
- Add products to cart from catalog
- View promotional offers

---

### Screen 4: Checkout
**Purpose:** Collect shipping and payment information to complete the purchase.

**Key UI Components:**
- Header: "Checkout" with navigation (Home | Products | Carts)
- "Shipping Information" form section with fields:
  - Full Name
  - Address
  - City
  - State
  - Zip
- "Payment Method" section with dropdown selector
- Payment card fields:
  - Card Number
  - Expiration Date
- Order Total display ($1052)

- "Cancel" button

**Main User Interactions:**
- Enter shipping address

---

### Screen 5: Order Confirmation
**Purpose:** Confirm successful order placement and provide order details.

**Key UI Components:**
- Header: "Order Confirmation" with navigation (Home | Products | Cart)
- Success message: "Your order has been placed successfully"
- "Order Summary" section displaying:

## B. Functional Requirements

### Product Browsing & Discovery

**FR-1:** The system shall display a home page with featured products, each showing product name, price, and an "Add to Cart" button.

**FR-2:** The system shall provide category-based navigation allowing users to filter products by categories including Laptops, Headphones, and Gaming.

**FR-3:** The system shall display promotional offers on the home page, including discount percentages and eligibility criteria.

**FR-4:** The system shall provide a product search functionality allowing users to search for products by entering search terms.

**FR-5:** The system shall display a product slideshow or carousel on the products page to showcase featured items.

**FR-6:** The system shall display shipping promotions (e.g., "Free shipping on orders over $100") on relevant pages.

---

### Shopping Cart Management

**FR-7:** The system shall allow users to add products to their shopping cart from the home page, products page, and featured product listings.

**FR-8:** The system shall display cart contents in a tabular format showing product name, quantity, individual price, and total price for each item.

**FR-9:** The system shall allow users to remove individual items from the shopping cart.

**FR-10:** The system shall automatically calculate and display subtotal, tax, and total amounts for all items in the cart.

**FR-11:** The system shall provide a "Continue Shopping" option from the cart page to return users to product browsing.

**FR-12:** The system shall provide a "Proceed to Checkout" button to advance users from the cart to the checkout process.

---

### Checkout & Payment

**FR-13:** The system shall collect shipping information including full name, address, city, state, and zip code during checkout.

**FR-14:** The system shall provide a payment method selector allowing users to choose their preferred payment option (with dropdown interface).

**FR-15:** The system shall collect payment card information including card number and expiration date.

**FR-16:** The system shall display the order total amount on the checkout page before order submission.

**FR-17:** The system shall allow users to cancel the checkout process and return to shopping.

**FR-18:** The system shall validate and process the order when the user clicks "Place Order."

---

### Order Confirmation & Management

**FR-19:** The system shall generate a unique order number for each successfully placed order.

**FR-20:** The system shall display an order confirmation page showing order number, purchased items, total paid, payment status, estimated delivery date, and shipping address.

**FR-21:** The system shall provide order confirmation with payment status indication (e.g., "Approved").

**FR-22:** The system shall calculate and display an estimated delivery date for confirmed orders.

## D. Ambiguities, Missing Information, and Assumptions

### Ambiguities:
1. **User Authentication:** No login/registration screens are shown. It's unclear if users must authenticate or can checkout as guests.
2. **Quantity Modification:** Cart shows quantities but no interface for changing them (only removal is shown).
3. **Product Details:** No dedicated product detail page is shown; unclear how users view full product specifications.
4. **Payment Methods:** Dropdown shown but specific payment options (credit card, PayPal, etc.) are not detailed.
5. **Tax Calculation:** Tax amount shown but calculation method (percentage, location-based) is not specified.

### Missing Information:
1. **Error Handling:** No indication of validation errors, payment failures, or out-of-stock scenarios.
2. **Order History:** No interface for viewing past orders.
3. **Account Management:** No user profile, saved addresses, or payment methods.
4. **Inventory Management:** No indication of stock levels or availability.
5. **Navigation Flow:** Unclear how "Products" link differs from category browsing.
6. **Email Confirmation:** No mention of order confirmation emails.

### Assumptions Made:
1. **Navigation Consistency:** Assumed the header navigation (Home | Products | Cart) is present on all screens.
2. **Cart Persistence:** Assumed cart contents persist across navigation between screens.
3. **Single Payment Method:** Assumed one payment method per order based on dropdown interface.
4. **Automatic Tax Calculation:** Assumed system calculates tax automatically based on subtotal.
5. **Order Processing:** Assumed "Place Order" triggers immediate payment processing and order creation.
6. **Guest Checkout:** Assumed users can complete purchases without creating an account.
7. **Product Availability:** Assumed all displayed products are in stock and available for purchase.
8. **Currency:** Assumed all prices are in USD based on "$" symbol usage.
