## A. Screen Descriptions

### Screen 1: Home Page (Landing)
**Purpose:** Serve as the main entry point for the electronics store, showcasing promotional content, featured products, and navigation options.

**Key UI Components:**
- Site header with "Electronics Store" branding
- Navigation menu: Home | Products | Cart
- Welcome banner with promotional text
- "Shop Now" call-to-action button
- "Shop by Category" section with category buttons (Laptops, Headphones, Gaming)
- "Featured Products" section displaying product cards (Product 1-3 with prices $49.99, $49.99, $49.99)
- "Add to Cart" buttons for each featured product
- "Special Offer" banner: "Get 30% off on selected items"
- "View Deals" button

**Main User Interactions:**
- Navigate between pages using header menu
- Browse products by category
- Add featured products to cart
- View special offers/deals

## B. Functional Requirements

### Navigation & Site Structure

**FR-1:** The system shall provide a persistent navigation menu on all screens containing links to Home, Products, and Cart pages.

**FR-2:** The system shall allow users to navigate between screens using the header navigation menu without losing cart contents.

---

### Product Browsing & Display

**FR-3:** The system shall display featured products on the home page with product name, price, and an "Add to Cart" button for each item.

**FR-4:** The system shall provide category-based browsing with at least three product categories (Laptops, Headphones, Gaming) accessible from the home page.

**FR-5:** The system shall display a product catalog on the Products page showing product name, price, and "Add to Cart" functionality for each item.

**FR-6:** The system shall provide a product search feature allowing users to search for products by entering search terms.

**FR-7:** The system shall display promotional information including special offers and shipping policies on relevant pages.

---

### Shopping Cart Management

**FR-8:** The system shall allow users to add products to their shopping cart from both the home page and products page.

**FR-9:** The system shall display all selected items in the shopping cart with product name, quantity, individual price, and total price for each item.

**FR-10:** The system shall allow users to remove individual items from the shopping cart.

**FR-11:** The system shall calculate and display the subtotal, tax amount, and total order amount in the shopping cart.

**FR-12:** The system shall provide a "Continue Shopping" option from the cart page to return users to product browsing.

---

### Checkout & Payment

**FR-13:** The system shall collect shipping information including full name, address, city, state, and zip code during checkout.

**FR-14:** The system shall provide a payment method selection mechanism with at least one payment option (card payment indicated by dropdown).

**FR-15:** The system shall collect payment card information including card number and expiration date when card payment is selected.

**FR-16:** The system shall display the order total amount on the checkout page before order submission.

**FR-17:** The system shall allow users to cancel the checkout process and return to the previous screen.

---

### Order Processing & Confirmation

**FR-18:** The system shall generate a unique order number upon successful order placement.

**FR-19:** The system shall display an order confirmation page showing order number, purchased items, total paid, payment status, estimated delivery date, and shipping address.

**FR-20:** The system shall provide a "Continue Shopping" option from the order confirmation page to allow users to browse additional products.

## D. Ambiguities, Missing Information & Assumptions

### Ambiguities:

1. **Quantity Management:** Screen 2 shows quantity column but no mechanism to adjust quantities (only remove option visible)
2. **Product Slideshow:** Screen 3 mentions "Product Slideshow" but no details on content or interaction
3. **Payment Method Options:** Only card payment fields shown; dropdown suggests multiple options but alternatives unclear
4. **Tax Calculation:** Tax amount shown but calculation method/rate not specified
5. **Category Navigation:** Unclear if category buttons filter products on same page or navigate to separate views
6. **Search Functionality:** No indication of search results display or filtering behavior

### Missing Information:

1. **User Authentication:** No login/registration functionality visible in any screen
2. **Product Details:** No product detail page shown; unclear how users view full product information
3. **Inventory Management:** No indication of stock availability or out-of-stock handling
4. **Error Handling:** No validation messages or error states shown for forms
5. **Order History:** No way to view past orders after confirmation
6. **Payment Processing:** No indication of payment gateway integration or processing states
7. **Shipping Options:** Only one shipping method implied; no selection mechanism shown
8. **Email Confirmation:** No indication of email receipt or notifications

### Assumptions Made:

1. **Single Currency:** All prices assumed to be in USD based on $ symbol
2. **Guest Checkout:** Assumed system allows checkout without user account (no login shown)
3. **Automatic Tax Calculation:** System calculates tax automatically based on shipping address
4. **Persistent Cart:** Cart contents persist across navigation between pages
5. **Single Shipping Address:** Only one shipping address per order supported
6. **Immediate Payment:** Payment processed immediately upon order placement
7. **Fixed Shipping Threshold:** Free shipping promotion applies at $100 order value
8. **State Selection:** State field likely uses dropdown (common pattern) though shown as text input
