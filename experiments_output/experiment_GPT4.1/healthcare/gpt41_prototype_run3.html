Frequently Asked Questions
  • Q: How do I schedule an appointment?
    A: Select "Schedule Appointment" from the menu and fill out the form.
  • Q: Can I cancel an appointment online?
    A: Yes, use the My Appointments page and select the appointment to cancel.
  • Q: How will I receive confirmation?
    A: Confirmation appears immediately after booking on the confirmation screen.
